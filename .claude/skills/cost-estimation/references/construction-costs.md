|----------|-------------|---------------------|

| Precast concrete | $500-1,000 | Floors, walls, facades |

| Glulam beams | $800-1,500 per m3 | Spans, exposed structure |

## Construction Duration Benchmarks

### Typical Durations (Design to Completion)

| Building Type | Design | Procurement | Construction | Total |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Low-rise residential (20 units) | 4-6 months | 2-3 months | 8-12 months | 14-21 months |
| Mid-rise residential (100 units) | 6-9 months | 3-4 months | 14-20 months | 23-33 months |
| High-rise residential (200 units) | 8-12 months | 3-6 months | 24-36 months | 35-54 months |
| Office (10,000m2) | 6-9 months | 3-4 months | 12-18 months | 21-31 months |
| Retail center (5,000m2) | 4-6 months | 2-3 months | 8-12 months | 14-21 months |
| School (8,000m2) | 6-9 months | 3-4 months | 12-16 months | 21-29 months |
| Hospital (20,000m2) | 12-18 months | 4-6 months | 24-36 months | 40-60 months |

## Life-Cycle Cost Considerations

### Annual Operating Cost by Building Type

| Type | Operating Cost ($/m2/year) | Components |
|------|---------------------------|------------|
| Residential (standard) | $30-60 | Maintenance $15-25, energy $8-15, insurance $3-5, management $5-15 |
| Residential (luxury) | $60-120 | Above + concierge, amenities, higher maintenance |
| Office (Grade A) | $80-150 | Maintenance $20-40, energy $15-30, cleaning $10-20, security $10-25, management $15-30 |
| Retail | $40-80 | Varies widely by tenant responsibility |
| Hotel | $150-300 per room/year | High services, F&B, housekeeping |

### Replacement Cycles

| Element | Design Life | Replacement Cost (% of original) |
|---------|-------------|----------------------------------|
| Structure | 60-100 years | N/A |
| Roof membrane | 20-30 years | 100% of roof cost |
| Facades (curtain wall seals) | 25-35 years | 30-50% of facade cost |
| HVAC plant | 15-25 years | 80-100% |
| Elevators | 20-30 years | 70-90% |
| Electrical systems | 25-35 years | 60-80% |
| Interior fit-out | 7-15 years | 50-100% |
| Landscaping (hard) | 20-30 years | 60-80% |
| Roads / paving | 15-25 years | 40-60% (resurfacing) |
